Recommended Locations in Japan:

  • The Kiso Valley

    In the Nagano Prefecture, the Kiso Valley is along the mountains of the Central Alps. The Valley got its name in ancient times for being a trade route called, Kisoji, which proved to be a vital to the business in the area.

  • Arashiyama

    On the Western outskirts of Kyoto. This location is most popular during the cherry blossom and fall seasons. The word 'Arashiyama'refers to the mountains on the southern bank of the river but it's also used to the name the entire district.

  • Nara Park

    A large park in central Nara, which the main attraction however it is home to hundreds of free roaming deer. You can even feed them, although they don't like to be teased with food, so definitely don't do that.
